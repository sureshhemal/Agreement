\subsection{Delivery quality and testing}
%-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  \item \textbf{End-to-end testing.} The Contractor will not perform end-to-end testing under this Agreement. If end-to-end testing is required, it will be charged separately at a rate to be agreed (not the LKR 4,000 per hour rate under this Agreement).

  \item \textbf{Code quality.} The Contractor is responsible for delivering maintainable, clean code, including unit tests.

  \item \textbf{QA.} The Contractor is not responsible for QA (quality assurance) work. QA is the Client's responsibility or to be agreed and charged separately if the Contractor is to perform it.

  \item \textbf{Documentation.} The Contractor shall deliver a technical document and a user manual for the application, to an agreed standard.
