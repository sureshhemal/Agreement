\section{Amendment --- All-in-one plans}
%-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.6}

\textbf{Invoice:} Each plan is invoiced monthly at the stated price.

\textbf{Plan charges:} The stated plan charges may be changed in the future. Any such change may reflect, among other factors, changes in the USD exchange rate. The Client will be notified of any change at least 30 calendar days in advance (or as otherwise agreed between the parties).
