\subsection{Governing law and dispute resolution}
%-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  \item \textbf{Governing law.} This Agreement is governed by, and shall be construed in accordance with, the laws of Sri Lanka.

  \item \textbf{Dispute resolution.} Before either party refers any dispute to court, the parties shall first try to resolve it in good faith (e.g.\ by discussion between the designated contacts). If the dispute is not resolved within a reasonable period, the parties shall attempt to resolve it through mediation before pursuing litigation.

  \item \textbf{Amendments.} Any change to this Agreement must be in writing and agreed by both parties.
